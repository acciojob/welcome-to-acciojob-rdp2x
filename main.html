Welcome to Accio Jobs!

AccioJob is an ed-tech startup founded by IIT Delhi alumni, designed to equip students and graduates with in-demand tech skills like Full Stack Web Development and Data Analytics. Their programs are structured to be beginner-friendly and are available both online and offline, with centers in cities like Noida, Hyderabad, and Pune.One of AccioJob's standout features is its strong placement support. They collaborate with over 500 hiring partners and conduct more than 60 hiring drives each month. Their students have secured positions in top tech companies, with the highest reported salary being 41 LPA and an average salary of 7.4 LPA. AccioJob offers a pay-after-placement model, meaning students are required to pay the course fee only after securing a job. The courses are taught by experienced instructors and mentors from top MNCs. The curriculum includes hands-on projects, mock interviews, and personalized mentorship to ensure students are job-ready.
